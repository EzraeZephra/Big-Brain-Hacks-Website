About Us

The Big Brain Hacks team is a group of like-minded students who pursue excellence in the field of Computer Science. In order to do this, we participate in coding tournaments known as "Hackathons" to gauge and improve our skill level and win prizes against other student teams. By joining our team, we aim to improve members' critical thinking, problem solving, and teamwork skills through combined efforts during and before tournaments. However, before joining the club, applicants are expected to display a general knowledge of computer programming. Entry will be determined by the current team members.
